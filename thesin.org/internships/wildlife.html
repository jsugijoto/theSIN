Wildlife Center of Silicon Valley

  Volunteers are the backbone of our organization. From animal care to data entry, volunteers keep the Wildlife Center and our animals alive! Whatever volunteer opportunity you choose, your contribution of time makes a BIG difference and helps give wildlife a second chance. The Wildlife Center is a great place for people who are passionate about wildlife conservation, and for those interested in gaining experience toward an animal-related career. Volunteering opportunities include in center care, transporting animals, community outreach, office support, fundraising, writing newsletters, and repair work.
