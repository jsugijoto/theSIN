THE STUDENT INTERNSHIP NETWORK DIFFERENCE


The Student Internship Network is a nonprofit organization dedicated to giving students the resources needed to get involved and gain experience in internships, volunteering, and unique collegiate courses of various fields of study. We help students find interesting and rewarding summer and year-round programs giving them the best chance at achieving their career goals. And if you are looking to open up new internship opportunities, recruit, or mentor in the Bay Area, we can coordinate that process as well! Sign up today to take the first step to your future!

Internships and Research

FAQ

The best way to figure out which internship to apply for is to look for one that interests you the most. The key is to find an internship that closely aligns to the career path you want to take.
Internships/volunteer activities allow you to put your education to test. Everything that you learn at school can be applied to the project. If you take part in an internship that aligns with your major, you will be able to figure out if that is the career path you want to take based on your experience. Internships/volunteer activities also look great on college applications because the college will be able to see how passionate you are regarding your major.
