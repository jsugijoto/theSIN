MISSION STATEMENT

Helping Students Achieve Their Goals

The Student Internship Network gives students the resources for them to succeed in pursuing their career goals. We provide college counseling, tutoring, and summer program opportunities to give you the best chance at achieving your career goals. We also aim to help promote valuable and effective internships and volunteering programs. Our success is a result of our commitment to the best people, the best solutions and the best results. We are committed to providing you the best possible experience.

More

If you are a program director, employer, or a professional in your field and wish to foster high school students in an internship or volunteering setting, email us about your business or program.
